Dashboard

Welcome to the Sky ERP Dashboard — your centralized view of all operational modules including Sales, Purchases, Inventory, Accounts, HR, Projects, CRM, and more. Get real-time insights, control, and connectivity — all in one place.

Why Choose Sky ERP Dashboard?

The Sky ERP dashboard connects every module of your business under one screen — giving you control, visibility, and data to make fast, smart decisions.

    Dashboard Modules & Features

  • Accounts Dashboard:- Monitor company finances in real-time — from receivables and payables to profit & loss and general ledger reports. Quickly analyze cash flow, tax liabilities, and financial KPIs with dynamic charts and filters.
  • Sales Dashboard:- Track the entire sales cycle — view quotations, orders, invoices, and collections. Analyze sales performance by product, region, or team. Set targets and compare actuals with beautiful graphs and reports.
  • Purchase Overview:- Manage vendors, purchase orders, pricing, and incoming shipments. Control budgets, review outstanding supplier bills, and automate approval workflows for smooth procurement operations.
  • HR & Payroll:- Automate attendance, leave applications, appraisals, payroll processing, and salary slips. Maintain employee records and documents while staying compliant with payroll regulations and taxation rules.
  • CRM Summary:- Capture leads from multiple sources, manage sales pipelines, and schedule customer follow-ups. Gain visibility into win/loss ratios, conversion rates, and salesperson activity using real-time dashboards.
  • Inventory Summary:- Get detailed reports on stock levels across warehouses, item valuation, reorder alerts, stock expiry, and batch/serial tracking. Enable FIFO, LIFO, or moving average inventory valuations as per need.
  • Projects Overview:- Plan and manage project tasks, milestones, budgets, and billing. Track time logs, resource allocation, and costs. Collaborate via shared timelines, Gantt charts, and centralized documentation.
  • Asset Management:- Register fixed assets, schedule preventive maintenance, calculate depreciation automatically, and manage asset transfers or disposals. Maintain location history, custodian details, and warranty status.
  • Manufacturing Insights:- Oversee BOMs, production planning, job cards, work orders, and raw material availability. Optimize manufacturing costs and timelines while tracking workstations and machine performance.
