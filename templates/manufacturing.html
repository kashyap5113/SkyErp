Core Manufacturing Modules

Bill of Materials (BOM):-

Define and manage components, operations, and standard costs required to manufacture a product. Easily configure multi-level BOMs to reflect real-world product complexity.

Production Planning:-

Schedule production runs based on demand forecasts and raw material availability. Optimize capacity and minimize delays by aligning resources with production goals.

Work Orders:-

Automatically generate work orders from sales or inventory needs. Track production progress, assign responsibilities, and ensure timely order fulfillment through structured workflows.

Job Cards:-

Break down work orders into specific tasks assigned to operators or machines. Monitor time spent, productivity, and job completion to ensure accountability on the shop floor.

Downtime Entry:-

Record and analyze unplanned machine downtimes or idle periods. Use this data to identify operational inefficiencies, root causes, and take corrective action to reduce future disruptions.

Workstation Type:-

Define and categorize various workstation types based on functionality and usage. Optimize layout, improve task allocation, and streamline workflows across the manufacturing line.

Reports:-

Access insightful reports covering production efficiency, machine usage, cost analysis, and overall performance. Use data to improve decision-making and drive continuous improvement initiatives.

Plant Floor:-

Get real-time visibility into production activities, WIP, and operator performance. Monitor key metrics directly from dashboards to manage floor operations efficiently.
