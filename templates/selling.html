Core Selling Modules

Quotation:- Create and share professional quotations quickly. Track revisions, convert quotations into orders, and manage customer negotiations seamlessly.

Sales Order:- Turn approved quotations into sales orders with a single click. Monitor order status, deliveries, and payments while ensuring accuracy in every transaction.

Sales Invoice:- Generate invoices instantly from sales orders or deliveries. Automate billing, manage payments, and maintain compliance with tax rules.

Point of Sales (POS):- Run your retail operations with an easy-to-use POS system. Process walk-in sales, manage discounts, and print receipts while syncing inventory in real time.

Sales Taxes & Charges Templates:- Automate tax calculations and apply predefined charges across invoices and quotations. Ensure compliance and reduce manual errors.

Sales Analytics:- Gain actionable insights with real-time reports on sales trends, revenue, customer behavior, and product performance. Improve decision-making with accurate data.

Sales Funnel:- Visualize the customer journey from lead to conversion. Identify bottlenecks, track deal progress, and optimize your sales strategy for higher conversions.

Reports:- Access detailed sales reports including order history, invoices, tax reports, and revenue forecasts to keep track of business performance.
