Accounting

Our Accounting module provides complete control over your financial operations with accuracy, automation, and compliance. From invoices and payments to tax management and reporting, it streamlines all accounting workflows into one secure and easy-to-use system. Whether you’re a small business or a growing enterprise, our Accounting solution ensures clarity in numbers, better decision-making, and faster growth.

Why Choose our Accounting Module?

Our Accounting Module delivers end-to-end automation of financial workflows, reducing manual effort and errors. With real-time dashboards and insightful reports, you gain complete visibility into your organization’s finances. Role-based access ensures sensitive data is always secure and compliant. Scalable and fully integrated with HRMS, CRM, Payroll, and other business modules, it adapts seamlessly to startups, SMEs, and large enterprises alike.

    Core Accounting Modules

  • Chart of Accounts:- A structured framework of all accounts that organizes assets, liabilities, income, and expenses for clear financial tracking.

  • Company:- Manage financial data for multiple companies or branches within a single system while keeping records separate and compliant.

  • Journal Entry:- Record all business transactions accurately with customizable templates to ensure transparency and audit readiness.
  • General Ledger:- The core of accounting, consolidating all financial entries into a centralized ledger for real-time insights and reporting.
  • Payment Entry:- Simplify payments and receipts by recording customer and vendor transactions with automated reconciliation.
  • Tax Report:- Generate accurate, compliant tax reports that streamline filing and regulatory submissions with ease.
  • Tax Types:- Define and manage multiple tax types such as GST, VAT, or TDS to suit your business needs.
  • Tax Category:- Categorize products and services under specific tax categories for automated calculation and compliance.
  • Tax Rules:- Configure custom tax rules and conditions to ensure precise application of taxes across transactions.
