Stock Inventory

The Stock Module helps businesses efficiently manage inventory, track stock movements, and maintain accurate levels across multiple warehouses. It ensures transparency, minimizes shortages or overstocking, and supports smooth operations with real-time data.

Why Choose Our Stock Module?

The Stock Module provides centralized control over inventory across multiple warehouses with real-time visibility into stock levels and valuation. It helps businesses avoid stockouts and over-purchasing by maintaining accurate records at all times. With built-in support for batch, serial number, and expiry tracking, it ensures precise monitoring of perishable and serialized items. Seamless integration with Purchase, Sales, and Accounting modules keeps the entire supply chain connected and efficient.

Core Stock Modules

Item Catalogue:-

Centralized list of all items with detailed specifications. Ensures consistency and easy reference for stock management.

Material Request:-

Allows teams to request materials as needed. Streamlines approvals and ensures timely availability of stock.

Stock Entry:-

Records all stock movements such as issues, receipts, transfers, and adjustments. Maintains accurate inventory records.

Purchase Receipt:-

Logs goods received from suppliers before invoicing. Ensures stock updates without impacting financials directly.

Delivery Note:-

Tracks items dispatched to customers against sales orders. Keeps stock levels updated and customer deliveries accurate.

Stock Ledger:-

Maintains a chronological record of all stock transactions. Enhances transparency and supports audits.

Stock Balance:-

Displays real-time stock availability by item and warehouse. Helps plan procurement and avoid stockouts.
