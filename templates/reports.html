Reports & Analytics

Unlock powerful insights with Sky ERP's built-in reports. From finance and HR to inventory, sales, and project tracking, access real-time, exportable reports and dashboards to make data-driven decisions quickly and effectively.

Reporting Features

Sky ERP provides a robust reporting engine that allows you to view, filter, customize, and export reports from any module. Get PDF, Excel, or printable formats instantly.

    Report Types & Features

  • Financial Reports:- Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Trial Balance, General Ledger, Accounts Receivable & Payable Ageing, and more — always in sync with your accounting entries.
  • Sales Reports:- Sales Order Trends, Customer-wise Sales, Item-wise Sales Summary, Sales Funnel, Conversion Rates, and Quotation analysis.
  • Purchase Reports:- Supplier-Wise Purchase, Item Purchase History, Pending Orders, and Monthly Purchase Summary.
  • Inventory Reports:- Stock Ledger, Stock Balance, Item-Wise Stock Ageing, Bin Reports, and Warehouse Transfers.
  • HR & Attendance:- Leave Balance Summary, Attendance Register, Monthly Attendance, Salary Register, and Employee Performance Reports.
  • CRM Reports:- Lead Conversion, Opportunity Pipeline, Customer Acquisition, Sales Team Activity Reports.
  • Project Reports:- Time Logs, Costing Sheets, Project Gantt Charts, Resource Utilization, and Task Progress Reports.
  • Manufacturing Reports:- Production Efficiency, Workstation Load, BOM Consumption, Job Card Analysis, and Scrap Tracking.
  • Asset Reports:- Asset Depreciation Ledger, Asset Movement History, Maintenance Logs, and Disposal Summary.
  • Custom Reports & Builder:- Create your own reports using ERPNext’s Report Builder or Script Reports for tailored insights.
