Employee Lifecycle Management

Track every stage of an employee's journey—from joining to exit. Maintain digital records of personal details, job history, documents, appraisals, and resignation formalities. Ensure smooth onboarding and structured offboarding.

Recruitment

Automate the hiring process. Publish job openings, shortlist candidates, schedule interviews, and roll out offers from within the system. Streamlined recruitment ensures you hire the right talent, faster.

Shift & Attendance

Manage shifts, rosters, and real-time attendance through biometric or manual entry. Get automated timesheets, late arrival reports, and overtime calculations—all synced with payroll.

Expense Claims

Simplify employee reimbursements. Employees can submit travel, food, or project-related claims with receipts. Managers approve digitally, ensuring faster settlements with complete audit trails.

Leave Management

Give employees the flexibility to request leave through a self-service portal. HR and managers can approve, reject, or track leave balances instantly. Configure multiple leave types with carry-forward, encashment, or custom policies.

Payroll & Salary Payout

Automate payroll generation with accuracy. Define salary structures, tax slabs, and deductions. Process salary slips, manage incentives, retention bonuses, and generate statutory reports—without errors or delays.

Performance Management

Set performance goals, assign KPIs, and run appraisal cycles. Capture 360° feedback and performance reviews to recognize top performers and plan growth paths.

Tax & Benefits

Handle statutory compliance with ease. Manage income tax slabs, deductions, PF, ESI, and other employee benefits with automated calculations and reporting.

Reports & Analytics

Access real-time dashboards for workforce insights. From salary registers to leave balances and performance trends, make data-driven HR decisions.
